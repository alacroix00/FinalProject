Fine Dining Finder

About Us

Fine Dining Finder allows users to search for over 1.5 million restaurants across 10,000 cities globally. Through the use of the Zomato API, Python, HTML/CSS, and Flask framework, we built an interactive website to filter and sort results depending on the user's input. As food connoisseurs, this was the perfect way for us to combine several of our interests and build an app with real-life utility. Our website has the ability to help anyone looking for a place to dine!
